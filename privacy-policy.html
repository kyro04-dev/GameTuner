Privacy Policy — Game Tuner

This Privacy Policy explains how Game Tuner handles information when you use the application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how data is used.

1. Information Collection

Game Tuner does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our own servers. The app does not require user registration or accounts, and all app activity occurs locally on the device.

However, the app integrates third-party advertising services that may collect certain non-personal information as described below.

2. Permissions Used

The app may use the following Android permissions:

  • QUERY_ALL_PACKAGES – Allows the app to display installed applications.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE – Allows detection of network status.
  • INTERNET – Required for loading third-party advertising content.
  • AD_ID – Allows access to the device Advertising ID for advertising purposes.

3. Third-Party Services (Advertising)

Game Tuner uses Google AdMob as its primary advertising platform. Ads may be served through AdMob and its mediation system. As part of ad mediation, Unity Ads is used as a bidding partner.

These advertising services may collect and process non-personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information (such as device model and operating system version)
  • Approximate location (derived from IP address)
  • Ad interaction and performance data

Data collected by these services is handled according to their respective privacy policies. Game Tuner does not control how third-party advertising providers collect or process data.

Third-Party Privacy Policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
    https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • Google Ads / AdMob Policy
    https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads
  • Unity Ads Privacy Policy
    https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy

4. Advertising

The app may display advertisements provided by Google AdMob and its mediation partners, including Unity Ads. Ads may be personalized or non-personalized depending on your device settings, regional requirements, and consent choices.

You can manage or limit personalized advertising by adjusting your Advertising ID settings on your Android device.

5. Children’s Privacy

This app is intended for users aged 13 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Security

Game Tuner does not store or transmit personal information to its own servers. Any data processed by advertising providers follows their respective security practices.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The updated date at the top of this page reflects the latest version.
